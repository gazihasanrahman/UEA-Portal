School of Computing Sciences

For more than 40 years we have been recognised internationally for excellence in our teaching, research and enterprise. We are very aware of the needs of industry and this is reflected in the development and application-led focus of our courses and research. We pride ourselves in giving all our students the necessary mentoring, support and skills to enable them to gain rewarding and high quality jobs. We are experts in areas such as Graphics Vision and Speech Analysis; Colour; Computational Biology; Data Science; Artificial Intelligence and growing focus on Cloud Computing/Virtualisation, Internet Technologies and Cyber Security.
